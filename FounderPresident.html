Mr. Shashikant Sutar

The Founder President
Raja Shree Shivraya Pratishthan

Hon'ble Mr. Shashikant Sutar, the Founder-President, popularly known as Bhau by all, has focused his energies on Social Work, Education and Culture. Raja Shree Shivraya Pratishthan runs successfully a range of educational institutes such as English and Marathi medium schools, D.Ed. colleges, Computer Science and Management colleges, Old People's home and Health clubs.

Bhau has been a friend and supporter of upliftment of people around, and is aptly known as the 'Architect of Kothrud'. His kind and understanding ways have endeared him to all.

New India School, at the behest of Bhau, has embraced all round development through deep-rooted cultural identity, national integrity and love for the nation.
