About Our School

School Name: DPS Ruby Park School

Established: 2003

Location: 254 Shanti Pally, R. B. Connector, Kolkata - 700107

DPS Ruby Park School is one of the leading educational institutions in Kolkata, committed to fostering academic excellence and nurturing well-rounded individuals. Since its inception in 2003, the school has continually strived to maintain the highest standards in both education and extracurricular activities. The campus boasts modern facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, science and computer labs, a vast library, and sports complexes. The school provides a safe and supportive learning environment where students can explore their potential and achieve their academic and personal goals. With a team of dedicated and experienced educators, DPS Ruby Park offers a holistic approach to education that prepares students for future challenges. The school also emphasizes the importance of values, discipline, and personal growth, ensuring that students graduate as responsible citizens of the world.
