Admission Procedure 2025-2026

An opportunity for those seeking admissions for the next academic year 2025-26. New India School, Rambaug colony welcomes you to visit our school campus. Get to know your child's school, school building, classrooms, playground, etc. before securing your child's admission.

Date : 2nd Dec. onwards (Monday to Friday).
Saturday : On prior appointment
Visiting hours : 9:00 am. to 11:00 am.

Admission procedure - Allotment of seats is subject to vacant seats. The Admission procedure will lasts till the vacant seats are filled.

Instructions

  • Please enable your Popup setting from Google Chrome.
  • Pre-Admission form fee of Rs. 600/- must also be paid online. This is mandatory to have access to the Pre-Admission form.
  • Once you fill the form online, click on the 'Submit' button. After submitting the form, the Print option for printing the form will appear.
  • If it does not appear, then Click on the Form link again and enter your mobile number. The already filled form number and a print icon will be shown.
  • Click on the Print icon and take a printout or save as a PDF file.
  • Take a printout of the Pre-admission form along with the form fee receipt, and hand it over to the school office the next day or within 5 days, between 8:30 am. and 3:00 pm. baring holidays.
  • School visit will be scheduled within 10 days after submission of the form to the school. The Date and Time of the school visit will be sent through Whatsapp message. Both the parents along with the child are expected during the School visit.
  • Note that only forms that are submitted physically to the school will be considered for the further admission procedure. Attested copy of Birth Certificate, Aadhar Card has to be submitted along with the printout of the submitted form.
  • Please note that the Form fee is not Transferable and Refundable.
  • In case of any queries, feel free to contact the school office on this number - 7350409424
